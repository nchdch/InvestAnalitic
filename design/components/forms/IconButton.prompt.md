Icon-only square button for toolbars, row actions and close — always give it a `label`.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Уведомления"><i data-lucide="bell" /></IconButton>
<IconButton variant="outlined" label="Настройки"><i data-lucide="settings" /></IconButton>
<IconButton variant="solid" size="lg" label="Отправить"><i data-lucide="arrow-up" /></IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost | outlined | solid`. Sizes: `sm | md | lg`. Pair with Lucide icon nodes.
