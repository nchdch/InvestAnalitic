Small status pill. Tone maps to a semantic role.

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent">ИИС</Badge>
<Badge tone="positive" dot>Выплачено</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" icon={<i data-lucide="alert-triangle" />}>Концентрация 28%</Badge>
<Badge tone="ai" icon={<i data-lucide="sparkles" />}>Совет ИИ</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | accent | positive | negative | warning | ai | outline`. Sizes `sm | md`. `dot` adds a status dot.
